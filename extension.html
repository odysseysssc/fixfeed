üéØ The 2-Step Process

First curate your "safe list" of accounts you actually engage with, then clean up everyone else.

STEP 1 Curate your list

1. Run your X archive through fixyourfeed.xyz to find your engaged accounts
2. Create a new X list (can be private)
3. Copy the handles from fixyourfeed, paste into the extension
4. Paste your list URL, hit Start
5. Extension adds them to your list automatically (5-8 sec each)

STEP 2 Clean up

1. Once Step 1 is complete, switch to "Clean up" tab
2. Paste the same list URL
3. Hit Start ‚Äî unfollows everyone NOT on your curated list
4. Your safe list accounts are protected, everyone else gets unfollowed

X limits how many actions you can take per day. The extension handles this automatically:

‚ùì FAQ

Is this safe? Will I get banned?

The extension uses X's own internal APIs with conservative rate limiting. Thousands of people use similar tools. That said, use at your own risk ‚Äî we can't guarantee anything with X's ever-changing policies.

Why does it take so long?

We intentionally slow things down (5-8 seconds between actions) to avoid triggering X's rate limits. Faster = more likely to get blocked.

It stopped and says "rate limited" ‚Äî what do I do?

Wait ~1 hour for unfollows, or until tomorrow for list adds. Then reopen the extension ‚Äî it saves your progress and will offer to resume where you left off.

Can I close the browser while it's running?

No ‚Äî you need to keep X open in a tab for the extension to work. But you can minimize it and do other things.

What if I accidentally unfollow someone I wanted to keep?

That's what Step 1 prevents! Make sure your curated list is complete before running Step 2. You can always re-follow people manually.

Does this work on Firefox/Safari?

Not yet ‚Äî only Chromium browsers (Chrome, Brave, Edge, Arc, etc.) for now.
